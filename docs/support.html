Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Block Unite?

Drag numbered blocks from the bottom selection area onto the 8x8 grid. When three or more identical blocks are placed next to each other, they merge into a higher number. Keep merging to reach 2048 and beyond!

How does the leaderboard work?

Your highest score is automatically submitted to the global leaderboard. Enter a nickname to appear on the leaderboard and compete with players worldwide. Internet connection required for leaderboard features.

What are gems used for?

Gems are earned by completing achievements and daily challenges. You can use gems to continue games when you run out of moves.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Block Unite works perfectly offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to sync your leaderboard scores and achievements.

How do daily challenges work?

New challenges are available every day! Complete them to earn bonus gems and exclusive achievements. Challenges reset at midnight UTC.

Is Block Unite free?

Yes, Block Unite is completely free to download and play. The game is supported by optional ads.

My game progress was lost. What should I do?

Game progress is saved locally on your device. Make sure you don't delete the app without backing up. If you're switching devices, your leaderboard nickname and score are saved in the cloud.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us using the email below with details about the bug, including your device model and iOS version. We appreciate your help in making Block Unite better!

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our players. Send us your ideas and feedback - we read every message.
