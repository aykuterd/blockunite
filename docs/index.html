How to Play

Master the block merging mechanics in a few simple steps

1

Drop Your Block

The upcoming block is displayed at the bottom-left as NEXT. Tap a column on the grid to drop the block into that column. Blocks fall to the lowest empty row in that column — just like Tetris!

2

Merge Identical Blocks

When two or more blocks with the same number touch each other (horizontally or vertically), they instantly merge into a single block with double the value. 2+2=4 → 4+4=8 → 8+8=16 → ... → 1024+1024=2048

3

Chain Merges for Big Points

A smart block placement can trigger a chain reaction! When blocks merge, the resulting block may match its new neighbors — triggering another merge automatically. Plan these chain reactions to multiply your points and clear board space!

4

Manage Your Board

Keep the grid from filling up by constantly creating merges. Spreading low-value blocks randomly will clog the board. Group similar numbers together to set up powerful chain merges and always keep rows open for new blocks.

5

Reach 2048 and Beyond!

Your ultimate goal is to merge blocks all the way up to 2048 — but don't stop there! The game continues until the grid is completely full with no adjacent matching blocks remaining. Push for the highest score possible!

Special Abilities

Powerful tools to turn the tide when you're in trouble — spend gems wisely!

↩️
UNDO

Regret your last move? Undo reverses your most recent block placement, giving you another chance to find a better spot on the board.

🗑️
TRASH

Discard the current block from your queue entirely. Perfect for getting rid of an inconvenient block and moving on to the next one.

💥
SMASH

Tap any block on the grid to destroy it and free up that space. Use it strategically to remove a block that's blocking a crucial merge opportunity.

🔀
MIX

Shuffle the upcoming blocks in your queue to randomize their order. Use this when none of the upcoming blocks seem helpful for your current board situation.
