🗑️ Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you would like to delete your data from Block Unite, please follow the instructions below.

📋 What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your nickname/display name
  • Your game scores and leaderboard entries
  • Your achievement progress
  • Your user ID from our database
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

⚠️ Important Notice

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted:

  • Your leaderboard ranking will be removed
  • All achievements and progress will be lost
  • You will need to create a new profile to play again
  • Your nickname may become available for other users
